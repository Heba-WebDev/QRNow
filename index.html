FAQ

What is a QR Code?

It's a machine-readable code consisting of an array of black and white squares, typically used for storing URLs or other information for reading by the camera on a smartphone.

Can I use it comercially?

Yes, the QR codes generated by the users are 100% free to be used both personally and comercially. Though, QRNow is not resposible for the content nor the usage of the codes.

Is QRNOw safe to use?

Yes. Creating and downloading QR Codes on QRNow is safe and secure. Privace is our main concern, that's why our service doesn't require any personal information.
